Kappa

Origin: Japanese Mythology

An amphibious, turtle-like humanoid found in the rivers and ponds. They have a water-filled cavity on their head, which is the source of their power. If the water is spilled, they become weak and vulnerable. Kappa are known for their mischievous behavior, but they can also be helpful if treated with respect.

Weakness: Bowing deeply; if they bow back, the water spills and they lose their strength.
