Sphinx

Origin: Egyptian Mythology

A creature with the head of a human and the body of a lion. In Greek legend, she terrorized Thebes by posing riddles to travelers and devouring those who could not answer. Oedipus famously solved her riddle, leading to her demise.

Weakness: Being outsmarted (if a traveler solves her riddle, she is defeated).
